CNVRT Support

Last updated: May 2026

Need help with CNVRT? Use the information below to contact support, report issues, or request help with your account.

Contact Support

Email: support@cnvrt.ai

Response time: We aim to respond within 24–48 hours.

What to Include

When contacting support, please include:

  • Your CNVRT account email
  • Your device type
  • Your iOS version
  • A short description of the issue
  • Screenshots or screen recordings, if helpful

Account Help

If you need help signing in, accessing your account, or deleting your account data, email us at support@cnvrt.ai.

Subscription Help

CNVRT subscriptions are managed through Apple. You can manage or cancel your subscription from your Apple ID account settings.

If you have questions about CNVRT Pro access, restoring purchases, or subscription status, contact support@cnvrt.ai.
